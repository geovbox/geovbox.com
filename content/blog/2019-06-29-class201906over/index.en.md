> Contents：This course primarily demonstrates the extension structural simulation method  
> Location：School of Geoscience and Engineering, Xianlin Campus, Nanjing University, A111 (Third Conference Room)  
> Time：June 28, 2019 (Friday) 9:30-17:30  
> Speaker：VBOX Developer: Li Changsheng  
> [Download the coursePPT](class2_20190628.pdf)



The course begins with an explanation of the principles of the discrete element method and an introduction to the usage of VBOX. In the practical session, 8 examples are demonstrated, and example scripts are provided.
8An example is：

- [Learn VBOX with one example](/example/ex1_push/) 
- [denudation](/example/ex2_syn_erosion/) 
- [sedimentation](/example/ex3_syn_sedimentaion/) 
- [fault](/example/ex4_pre_struct/) 
- [slip layer](/example/ex5_detachment/) 
- [paleo-uplift](/example/ex6_palaeohigh/) 
- [rigid substrate stretch structure](/example/ex7_extens_rigid/)
